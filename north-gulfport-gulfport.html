FAQS

What are some popular outdoor activities in North Gulfport?

Some popular outdoor activities in North Gulfport include fishing at the Gulfport Harbor, kayaking in the Gulf of Mexico, and hiking at the nearby Mississippi Sandhill Crane National Wildlife Refuge.

Are there any parks and recreational areas in North Gulfport?

Yes, North Gulfport offers several parks and recreational areas for visitors and residents to enjoy, such as Jeff Davis Park, North Gulfport Community Park, and the Seabee Memorial Park.

Where can I go shopping in North Gulfport?

North Gulfport features various retail hotspots for shopping enthusiasts, including the Gulfport Premium Outlets, Crossroads Shopping Center, and North Gulfport Plaza.

What are some accommodation options in North Gulfport?

Visitors to North Gulfport can choose from a range of hotels and lodging options, including the Fairfield Inn & Suites Gulfport, Comfort Suites Gulfport, and Holiday Inn Gulfport-Airport.

Are there any specific hotels and lodging facilities recommended in North Gulfport?

Some recommended hotels and lodging facilities in North Gulfport include the Courtyard by Marriott Gulfport Beachfront, Hampton Inn & Suites Gulfport, and Home2 Suites by Hilton Gulfport I-10.
