FAQS

How often should a commercial pool be cleaned?

A commercial pool should be cleaned at least once a week to maintain proper hygiene and water quality.

What is shock treating a pool and why is it important?

Shock treating a pool involves adding a concentrated dose of chemicals to kill bacteria, algae, and other contaminants in the water. It is important to shock treat the pool regularly to maintain clean and safe swimming conditions.

Can I use household cleaning products to clean a commercial pool?

No, household cleaning products are not suitable for cleaning a commercial pool as they may contain chemicals that can be harmful to swimmers. It is recommended to use commercial pool cleaning products that are specifically designed for pool maintenance.

How can I prevent algae buildup in a commercial pool?

To prevent algae buildup in a commercial pool, it is important to maintain proper water balance, regularly shock treat the pool, and clean the pool walls and floor to remove any algae spores.

What is the best way to remove dirt and debris from the bottom of a commercial pool?

The best way to remove dirt and debris from the bottom of a commercial pool is by vacuuming the pool regularly using a pool vacuum cleaner. This will help keep the pool clean and prevent buildup of dirt and particles.
