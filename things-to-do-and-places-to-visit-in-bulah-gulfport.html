Ship Island Excursions

If you're looking for a unique adventure during your visit to Gulfport, Mississippi, consider taking a trip to Ship Island. Located just off the coast, Ship Island is a beautiful barrier island that offers visitors the chance to explore pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and fascinating historical sites. Whether you're interested in sunbathing, snorkeling, birdwatching, or learning about the island's rich past, Ship Island has something for everyone.

One of the highlights of Ship Island excursions is the opportunity to visit Fort Massachusetts, a well-preserved brick fortress that dates back to the mid-19th century. History enthusiasts will appreciate the chance to learn about the fort's role in the Civil War and its significance in protecting the Gulf Coast. In addition to exploring the fort, visitors can enjoy picnicking on the beach, swimming in the refreshing waters, and spotting dolphins and other marine life off the shore. A day trip to Ship Island is a perfect way to experience the natural beauty and historical intrigue of the Gulfport area.

Institute for Marine Mammal Studies

The Institute for Marine Mammal Studies in Gulfport, Mississippi is a fascinating place to visit for all ages. With a focus on research, conservation, and education, the institute offers a unique opportunity to learn about marine life in the Gulf of Mexico. Visitors can explore interactive exhibits, attend educational programs, and even participate in hands-on experiences like dolphin encounters.

One of the highlights of a visit to the Institute for Marine Mammal Studies is the chance to see these magnificent animals up close. From playful dolphins to graceful sea turtles, the institute houses a variety of marine species that are sure to captivate visitors. By supporting the institute's mission of marine conservation and research, guests can actively contribute to the protection of these incredible creatures and the preservation of their habitats.

Jones Park

Jones Park is a beloved green space in Gulfport, Mississippi, offering a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. This sprawling park boasts lush landscapes, scenic walking paths, and serene picnic areas, making it a perfect spot for a leisurely day outdoors. Visitors can unwind amidst the tranquil surroundings, bask in the warm Mississippi sun, and enjoy the picturesque views of the Gulf Coast.

In addition to its natural beauty, Jones Park also features a playground area for children, making it an ideal destination for families looking to spend quality time together. The park hosts various community events throughout the year, adding to its vibrant atmosphere and providing entertainment for locals and tourists alike. Whether you're seeking a tranquil retreat or a fun-filled day out with loved ones, Jones Park offers a serene setting for relaxation and recreation in the heart of Gulfport.
