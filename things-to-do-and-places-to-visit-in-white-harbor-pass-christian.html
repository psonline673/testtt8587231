Mardi Gras Museum

Located in the heart of White Harbor, the Mardi Gras Museum offers visitors a fascinating glimpse into the rich history and vibrant culture of this iconic celebration. Step inside and immerse yourself in a world of colorful costumes, elaborate masks, and captivating displays that showcase the traditions and customs of Mardi Gras in all its glory. From intricately designed float designs to historical artifacts, the museum provides a comprehensive look at how this annual festival has become a cherished part of Pass Christian's identity.

As you wander through the museum's halls, you'll have the opportunity to learn about the origins of Mardi Gras, its significance to the local community, and the artistry that goes into creating the elaborate parade floats that grace the streets each year. Interactive exhibits and audio-visual presentations bring the history of Mardi Gras to life, making it a truly engaging experience for visitors of all ages. Whether you're a history buff, a fan of cultural celebrations, or simply looking to immerse yourself in the vibrant spirit of Mardi Gras, a visit to the Mardi Gras Museum is a must-do when exploring White Harbor.

Pass Christian Isles Golf Club

The Pass Christian Isles Golf Club is a haven for golf enthusiasts visiting White Harbor. Nestled along the Mississippi Gulf Coast, this picturesque golf course offers a challenging yet enjoyable experience for players of all skill levels. The lush green fairways, well-maintained bunkers, and scenic views of the Gulf of Mexico provide a serene backdrop for a round of golf.

Golfers can take advantage of the club's state-of-the-art facilities, including a pro shop stocked with top-of-the-line equipment and apparel. The on-site restaurant offers delicious food and refreshing drinks to enjoy after a game, making it the perfect place to relax and unwind with fellow players. Whether you are a seasoned golfer or a beginner looking to improve your skills, the Pass Christian Isles Golf Club is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a premier golfing experience in Mississippi.

War Memorial Park

War Memorial Park in Pass Christian, Mississippi, holds a special place in the hearts of locals and visitors alike. Situated along the calming shores of the Gulf of Mexico, this peaceful park is a tribute to the brave men and women who have served in the military. The park features beautifully maintained grounds, serene walkways, and touching memorials that honor the sacrifices made by those who have defended our country.

Visitors to War Memorial Park can take a moment to reflect and pay their respects at the various monuments scattered throughout the park. Additionally, the park offers a tranquil setting for picnics, relaxing walks, and even quiet moments of contemplation. Whether you have a personal connection to the military or simply appreciate the courage and dedication of those who serve, a visit to War Memorial Park is a moving and meaningful experience that shouldn't be missed during your time in Pass Christian.

The Blind Tiger Pass Christian Beach

The Blind Tiger at Pass Christian Beach is a popular dining spot known for its delicious seafood offerings and laid-back atmosphere. Situated right by the beach, you can enjoy your meal while taking in stunning views of the Gulf of Mexico. The restaurant's outdoor seating area is perfect for soaking up the sun and enjoying a refreshing drink.

In addition to its scenic location, The Blind Tiger is also well-loved for its menu that features a variety of fresh seafood dishes, from shrimp po'boys to seafood platters. Whether you're a seafood lover or just looking to relax by the beach with a tasty meal, The Blind Tiger at Pass Christian Beach is a must-visit spot in White Harbor.
