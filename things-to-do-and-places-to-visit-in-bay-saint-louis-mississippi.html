Hollywood Casino Gulf Coast

Located in Bay Saint Louis, Mississippi, Hollywood Casino Gulf Coast is a premier destination for those seeking entertainment and excitement. With a luxurious atmosphere and a wide range of amenities, this popular casino offers a diverse array of gaming options for visitors to enjoy. From slot machines to table games, there is something for everyone to experience the thrill of a win.

In addition to the gaming floor, Hollywood Casino Gulf Coast boasts several dining options to satisfy any craving. Whether you're in the mood for a casual meal or a fine dining experience, the on-site restaurants offer a variety of delicious dishes to choose from. After a day of gaming and dining, guests can relax and unwind in the comfortable accommodations provided by the casino's hotel, ensuring a memorable stay at Hollywood Casino Gulf Coast.

The Blind Tiger

Nestled along the picturesque shoreline of Bay Saint Louis, The Blind Tiger offers a delightful dining experience with a laid-back coastal vibe. This waterfront restaurant is a favorite among locals and visitors alike, providing a perfect spot to unwind and savor delicious food while enjoying stunning views of the bay.

The menu at The Blind Tiger features a delectable array of fresh seafood dishes, from mouthwatering shrimp po'boys to flavorful fish tacos. Pair your meal with a refreshing cocktail or a cold beer from their extensive drink selection. Whether you're looking for a casual lunch with friends or a romantic dinner for two, The Blind Tiger promises a memorable culinary experience in the heart of Bay Saint Louis.

100 Men Hall

100 Men Hall is a historic gem nestled in Bay Saint Louis, Mississippi. This iconic venue has been a cornerstone of the local community for decades, serving as a hub for music, art, and cultural events. The hall's rich history is palpable as soon as you step inside, with its walls echoing the melodies of blues, jazz, and soul that have reverberated within its walls for generations.

Stepping into 100 Men Hall is like stepping back in time, where you can feel the stories of the past come alive through the vibrant performances and events hosted there. From live music to art exhibitions, the hall continues to be a vibrant space where creativity and community intersect. Whether you're a music enthusiast, history buff, or simply looking to soak up the unique atmosphere of Bay Saint Louis, a visit to 100 Men Hall is a must during your stay in this charming Mississippi town.

100 Men Hall



Lighthouse Park

Lighthouse Park in Bay Saint Louis, Mississippi is a serene waterfront area that offers stunning views of the bay and the iconic historic lighthouse. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll along the paved pathways that wind through the park, enjoying the fresh sea breeze and the picturesque scenery. The park is a popular spot for picnics, with plenty of shaded areas and picnic tables available for visitors to relax and enjoy a meal while taking in the beautiful surroundings.

For those looking to get active, Lighthouse Park also features a playground for children and open spaces for recreational activities such as frisbee or soccer. The park is a favorite among locals and tourists alike, who flock to the area to soak up the sun, watch the boats go by, and maybe even catch a stunning sunset over the bay. Lighthouse Park is not just a park, but a peaceful retreat where visitors can truly unwind and appreciate the natural beauty of the Mississippi Gulf Coast.
