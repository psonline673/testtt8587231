University of Southern Mississippi Gulf Park Campus

The University of Southern Mississippi Gulf Park Campus is a beacon of higher education in the charming town of Long Beach, Mississippi. Nestled along the scenic Gulf Coast, this campus offers a unique academic experience that seamlessly blends coastal living with academic excellence. Students have the opportunity to pursue various undergraduate and graduate programs while enjoying the beautiful surroundings that inspire creativity and learning.

One of the standout features of the University of Southern Mississippi Gulf Park Campus is its state-of-the-art facilities that cater to the diverse needs of students. From modern classrooms to cutting-edge research labs, the campus provides a conducive environment for both academic pursuits and personal growth. Additionally, the dedicated faculty and staff ensure that students receive a top-notch education that prepares them for success in their chosen fields.

Bear Point Marina

Bear Point Marina is a bustling hub of activity in Long Beach, Mississippi. Situated along the beautiful Gulf Coast, this marina offers a picturesque setting for locals and visitors alike to enjoy. Boating enthusiasts can take advantage of the convenient access to the water, making it the perfect spot to launch a boat and spend a day out on the open sea.

In addition to its prime location for boating adventures, Bear Point Marina is also home to a variety of amenities to enhance your experience. From waterfront dining options serving up delicious seafood to shops where you can stock up on boating essentials, there is something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you're a seasoned sailor or just looking to soak in the scenic views, Bear Point Marina is a must-visit destination in Long Beach.

Long Beach Lookout Tower

One of the iconic landmarks in Long Beach, Mississippi is the Long Beach Lookout Tower. Standing tall and proud, this historic structure offers visitors a chance to take in breathtaking panoramic views of the Gulf Coast. As you climb to the top, the beauty of the coastline unfolds before your eyes, making it a must-visit spot for tourists and locals alike.

Originally built as a fire observation tower, the Long Beach Lookout Tower now serves as a popular tourist attraction. Whether you're looking to capture stunning photographs, relax and enjoy the sea breeze, or simply take a moment to appreciate the natural beauty of the area, this tower provides the perfect vantage point. Make sure to include a visit to the Long Beach Lookout Tower on your itinerary when exploring this charming coastal town.

Long Beach Lookout Tower



Town Green Park

Town Green Park in Long Beach, Mississippi, is a vibrant community space that offers a range of activities for locals and visitors alike. This well-maintained park features lush greenery, walking paths, and a playground for children, making it a perfect spot for families to unwind and enjoy the outdoors. With its picturesque setting and peaceful ambiance, Town Green Park provides a welcome escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

In addition to its natural beauty, Town Green Park also hosts various events and festivals throughout the year, attracting crowds from near and far. From music concerts to food festivals, there is always something exciting happening at this popular park. Whether you're looking to relax in the sun, engage in recreational activities, or simply soak up the local culture, Town Green Park is a must-visit destination in Long Beach.
