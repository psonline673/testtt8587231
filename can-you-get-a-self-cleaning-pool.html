Common Misconceptions About SelfCleaning Pools

One common misconception about self-cleaning pools is that they require no maintenance at all. While these pools do offer convenience and efficiency by automating some cleaning tasks, they still need regular monitoring and occasional manual cleaning to ensure optimal performance. Another misconception is that self-cleaning pools eliminate the need for professional pool cleaning services. While these pools can reduce the frequency of manual cleaning, it is still recommended to schedule regular professional cleanings to ensure proper maintenance and longevity of the pool. To optimize the self-cleaning functionality of your residential swimming pool, incorporating optimal water circulation techniques is imperative.
