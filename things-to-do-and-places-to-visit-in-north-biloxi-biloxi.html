OhrO'Keefe Museum of Art

The Ohr-O'Keefe Museum of Art in North Biloxi is a must-visit destination for art enthusiasts and history buffs alike. The museum is dedicated to showcasing the works of George E. Ohr, a renowned American ceramic artist known as the "Mad Potter of Biloxi." Visitors can explore the stunning collection of Ohr's pottery pieces, which are characterized by their unique shapes and vibrant colors that truly showcase his artistic genius.

Apart from the impressive display of Ohr's works, the museum also features rotating exhibitions that highlight various aspects of art and culture. The contemporary design of the museum complex itself is a work of art, with striking architectural elements that beautifully complement the surrounding landscape. Whether you're a fan of pottery, architecture, or simply appreciate fine art, a visit to the Ohr-O'Keefe Museum of Art promises a memorable and enriching experience in North Biloxi.

Biloxi Lighthouse

When visiting North Biloxi, the iconic Biloxi Lighthouse is a must-see attraction that captures the essence of this coastal city. Standing tall and proud, the lighthouse is a symbol of hope and resilience, having withstood the test of time, including the devastation of hurricanes. Its picturesque location along the Biloxi Beach Promenade offers visitors a serene spot to admire the views of the Gulf of Mexico and take in the fresh sea breeze.

Constructed in 1848, the Biloxi Lighthouse is one of the last cast-iron lighthouses in the United States and is steeped in maritime history. Climb up its spiral staircase to the top for a stunning panoramic view of the surrounding area, where you can appreciate the beauty of the Mississippi Gulf Coast. Whether you're a history buff, a photography enthusiast, or simply a traveler seeking a unique experience, a visit to the Biloxi Lighthouse will surely be a memorable highlight of your trip to North Biloxi.

Mississippi Coast Coliseum

Mississippi Coast Coliseum, located in the heart of North Biloxi, is a premier entertainment venue that hosts a wide range of events throughout the year. From concerts and sporting events to trade shows and conventions, there is always something exciting happening at the coliseum. With its seating capacity of over 15,000, the coliseum attracts both locals and visitors looking to experience top-notch entertainment.

One of the highlights of Mississippi Coast Coliseum is its state-of-the-art facilities that provide a comfortable and enjoyable experience for attendees. Whether you're catching a live performance by your favorite artist or attending a thrilling sports game, the coliseum offers excellent amenities such as concession stands, modern restrooms, and ample parking. Additionally, the coliseum's central location makes it easily accessible from various parts of Biloxi, making it a convenient choice for entertainment seekers in the area.

Keesler Air Force Base

Keesler Air Force Base is a significant military installation located in North Biloxi, Mississippi. Established in 1941, it serves as a major training facility for the United States Air Force. The base is named after 2nd Lt. Samuel Reeves Keesler Jr., a Mississippi native who lost his life in a plane crash during World War I.

Visitors to Keesler Air Force Base can explore the Aerospace Museum, which showcases the history and heritage of the Air Force. The base also offers various recreational facilities such as golf courses, swimming pools, and fitness centers for both military personnel and their families. Additionally, the annual Keesler Air Show attracts aviation enthusiasts from across the country, featuring thrilling aerial displays and ground exhibitions.
