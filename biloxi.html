From luxurious beachfront resorts to quaint bed and breakfasts, there is something for everyone in this vibrant coastal city. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway or a fun-filled family vacation, Biloxi has a plethora of accommodation choices to make your stay comfortable and memorable.

Visitors can choose to stay at one of the many upscale hotels and resorts that line the beautiful beaches of Biloxi. These establishments offer top-notch amenities, stunning ocean views, and world-class service that will make you feel pampered and relaxed throughout your stay. Additionally, for those seeking a more laid-back experience, charming guesthouses and cozy inns are scattered throughout the city, providing a cozy and homey atmosphere for travelers looking to immerse themselves in the local culture.

Outdoor Activities in Biloxi

When it comes to outdoor activities in Biloxi, there is no shortage of options to keep you entertained and active throughout your stay. With its stunning Gulf Coast location, Biloxi offers a paradise for fishing enthusiasts and golf lovers alike. The waters off the coast are teeming with a variety of fish, making it a prime spot for both experienced anglers and beginners looking to cast a line and reel in a catch.

In addition to fishing, golfing is a popular outdoor activity in Biloxi, with several top-notch courses dotting the city and surrounding areas. Whether you're a seasoned golfer looking to test your skills on a championship course or a novice player looking to improve your swing, Biloxi has a course to suit every level of expertise. Fishing and Golfing

For fishing enthusiasts, Biloxi is a paradise on the Gulf Coast. With access to the Gulf of Mexico and an array of inland waterways, there are ample opportunities for both saltwater and freshwater fishing. Whether you prefer deep-sea fishing for trophy catches like red snapper and grouper, or casting your line in the serene waters of the bayous for speckled trout and redfish, Biloxi offers a diverse range of options for anglers of all skill levels. Charter fishing trips are readily available for those looking to venture further offshore in search of prized game fish.

Golfers will find Biloxi to be a haven for their favorite pastime, with several top-notch golf courses scattered throughout the city and its surrounding areas. The warm climate and picturesque landscapes make for an ideal golfing experience year-round. From challenging championship courses designed by renowned architects to more casual public courses offering stunning views of the Gulf, there is no shortage of options for golfers looking to tee off in Biloxi. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner looking to improve your game, the golfing scene in Biloxi caters to all levels of players.

Transportation in Biloxi

Traveling around Biloxi is convenient thanks to the city's well-connected transportation options. Whether you choose to rent a car, hail a taxi, or hop on a bus, getting around the city is easy and efficient. Car rentals are available at the Gulfport-Biloxi International Airport as well as various locations throughout the city, providing visitors with the flexibility to explore at their own pace. Taxis are also a convenient option for those looking to travel quickly and comfortably between destinations. Additionally, the Coast Transit Authority offers several bus routes that connect key areas within Biloxi, making it an affordable and accessible choice for getting around town.

For those looking to explore beyond Biloxi, the city is conveniently located near major highways, including Interstate 10, making it easy to venture to neighboring towns and cities. If you prefer to leave the driving to someone else, ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are also readily available in Biloxi. Whether you're heading to the beach, exploring local attractions, or embarking on a day trip, the varied transportation options in Biloxi ensure that getting around is a hassle-free experience for visitors and locals alike.

Public Transit Options

For visitors looking to navigate Biloxi without a car, there are public transit options available in the city. The Coast Transit Authority (CTA) provides bus services throughout Biloxi and the surrounding areas, making it convenient for tourists to explore the city without the need for a personal vehicle. The CTA buses follow designated routes and schedules, offering a cost-effective and reliable way to travel around Biloxi.

In addition to the bus services, visitors can also opt for the Beachcomber Trolley, a popular mode of transportation along the Mississippi Gulf Coast. The trolley runs along the beachfront, providing passengers with scenic views as they travel between various attractions and entertainment venues. The Beachcomber Trolley offers a convenient and fun way to experience the beauty of Biloxi while getting around the city with ease.
